Food for thoughts - Functional Nutrition Substance over style!

It's incredibly important to change the paradigm associated with modern-day eating. There are more and more people with health issues stemming from metabolic issues. Most current diseases, from autoimmune diseases to neoplasms, can be eliminated or mitigated by dietary choices.

Why do we continue to offer croquettes, rissoles, cakes laden with tons of sugar, and other foods devoid of any nutritional value? Unless it's to satisfy a craving for a quick bite. And in this case, the 'craving' isn't the feeling of hunger; it's actually the 'craving' that ends up consuming these products.
